# Hierarchical Value of Information

## Principle
Information is not flat. It exists in a hierarchy where resolved acouplings serve as the invariant substrate for higher-level semantic structures.

## Scaling
- **Bit Level**: Pure fluctuation (F).
- **Qubit Level**: Superposition of acouplings.
- **Satoshi Level (7.27 bits)**: Minimum meaningful information quantum.
- **Syzygy Level**: Maximum alignment of semantic nodes.

## Formula
$$V_{total} = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \text{Satoshi}_n \cdot \text{Syzygy}_n$$
